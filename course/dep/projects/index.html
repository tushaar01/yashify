Our Mission

The Department of Microbiology and Biotechnology is dedicated to advancing scientific knowledge through cutting-edge research in microbial sciences, biotechnology applications, and innovative biological solutions. We strive to bridge the gap between fundamental research and practical applications that benefit society.

Key Objectives:

  • Excellence in microbiology and biotechnology research
  • Innovation in biological solutions and applications
  • Collaboration with industry and academia
  • Training future scientists and researchers

Developing novel solutions to complex biological challenges
