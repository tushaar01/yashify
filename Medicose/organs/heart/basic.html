Basic Heart Information

What? A muscular organ that pumps blood throughout the body
Size? About the size of your fist (250-350g)
Location? Left side of the chest, behind the ribcage
Chambers? 4 chambers (2 atria + 2 ventricles)
Beats per day? ~100,000 times (60-100 bpm)
Blood pumped daily? ~2,000 gallons (7,500 liters)

Deeper Heart Facts

Blood Circulation:
  • Pulmonary (heart ‚Üí lungs ‚Üí heart)
  • Systemic (heart ‚Üí body ‚Üí heart)
4 Valves:
  • Tricuspid & Mitral (AV valves)
  • Pulmonary & Aortic (semilunar valves)
Electrical System: SA node ‚Üí AV node ‚Üí Bundle of His ‚Üí Purkinje fibers
Warning Signs: Chest pain, shortness of breath, dizziness, fatigue
Healthy Heart Tips: Exercise, balanced diet, no smoking, stress management
