Heart

Basic Info

The heart is a muscular organ that pumps blood throughout the body, supplying oxygen and nutrients to tissues and removing carbon dioxide and other wastes.

Common Diseases

Heart attack, arrhythmia, coronary artery disease, cardiomyopathy, and heart failure are some common heart diseases.

Treatments

Common treatments include medications, lifestyle changes, surgical interventions like bypass surgery, and use of medical devices like pacemakers.

Research-Based Info

Recent research includes development of artificial hearts, stem cell therapy for damaged heart tissue, and advances in minimally invasive heart surgery.
